Ad Hominem

Being mean to someone or not believing them because of who they are or how they look.

Example: "His family is poor so he must not be smart. Don't believe anything he says."

No True Scotsman

Making unreasonable definitions about a specific group, and claiming someone is not truly part of the group unless they meet the definition.

Example: “You think you’re a Batman fan? Only a ‘true’ Batman fan has a signed poster!”

Bandwagon

Believing or doing something because many other people are doing it, even if it's wrong.

Example: “Everyone in class thinks the Vancouver Canucks are going to win the hockey game, so they definitely will.”

Appeal to Emotion

Making people feel a certain emotion in order for you to win the argument.

Example: “You have to eat that, think about the starving children in other countries!”
